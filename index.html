David Paiz-Torres

Freelance Journalist

David Paiz-Torres is a freelance journalist who specializes in data visualization. He has a passion for using data to tell compelling stories and make complex information accessible to a wide audience. David is skilled in data analysis, visualization, and storytelling, and has a knack for reading any story that incorporates scrollytelling.

David is a recent graduate from the Craig Newmark Graduate School of Journalism, with a Master's degree in Journalism, where he specialized in Local Accountability Reporting and Data Visualization. David has bylines in Tech Poliy Press and PCMag, which can be found here.
