About Me:

David Paiz-Torres is dedicated journalist with a passion for data-driven storytelling. He is currently pursuing a Master of Arts in Journalism with a specialization in Data Journalism at the Craig Newmark Graduate School of Journalism in New York, expected to graduate in December 2024.

While at the Newmark J-School, David has covered the impacts of inflation on Bronx residents, and union rallies following workplace layoffs

David has basic data skills to clean, analyze, and aggregate datasets. His previous analysis found insights such as the Bronx having the highest rate of maintenance-related inspection violations at child care facilities.

David also has basic HTML & CSS skills which he is currently expanding on through courses at the J-School.

Prior to his graduate studies, he earned my Bachelor's Degree in Media and Communications from the State University of New York at Old Westbury where he was a staff writer for the Old Westbury Catalyst. As a staff writer, he covered crime-related stories, and received recognition, including the Media Arts Celebration Award for Best News Story in 2023 and Best Opinion Article in 2022

Fluent in both English and Spanish, David possess technical skills in video and audio editing using tools such as Adobe Premiere, ProTools, and Adobe Audition.

David is excited to bring his diverse skills and experiences to future journalistic endeavors, continuing to tell impactful stories with accuracy and depth.
