A Year Into New York City's War on Rats,
Rodent Violations at NYC Schools Remain High


New York City Inspection data shows that not even schools are immune to New York City's ‘War on Rats.’
With hundreds of rodent-related violations reported in 2023

Mayor Eric Adams signs four pieces of legislation, declaring war on NYC rats. Nov. 2022

By: David Paiz-Torres
December 15, 2023


The Findings:

One year into New York City's war on rats, schools across the city continue to receive violations for rodent sightings during cafeteria inspections, according to Department of Health and Mental Hygiene data. Nearly a thousand violations were recorded throughout 2023 for rodents and roaches, collectively.

The borough with the highest rodent-violation rate: Brooklyn, according to the inspection data as of November 27, 2023.
